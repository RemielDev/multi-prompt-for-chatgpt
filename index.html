Distinct angles

Identical, "different approach", or distinct per-chat angles: concise, detailed, critical, creative, step-by-step.

Media-safe

Re-attaches images to every chat and refuses to submit if media is missing - so comparisons are fair.

Tidy workspace

1-10 chats per click, auto-grouped into one labeled tab group, with one-click close-all and shortcuts.
